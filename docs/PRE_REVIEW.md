## Reviewer lanes

### Empirical-methods / benchmarks review

**Expertise:** Baselines, generated figures, statistics, and threats to validity.

**Red-team questions:**
1. Are paper figures generated from real checked-in data?
1. Are baseline comparisons scoped to tools that can ingest the pair?
1. Do claims-audit checks keep the public prose within proven scope?
1. Are negative results and abstentions reported alongside wins?

**Evidence:** `figures`, `positioning`, `traceability`, `paper-source`

### PL / formal-methods soundness review

**Expertise:** Language semantics, product programs, mechanized proof boundaries.

**Red-team questions:**
1. Does the divergence definition match the source/target semantics claimed?
1. Are source-definedness, target-definedness, and consequence separated?
1. Are UNKNOWN/ABSTAIN frontiers loud enough to preserve one-sided soundness?
1. Do Lean/Coq checker claims line up with the executable oracle?

**Evidence:** `paper-source`, `traceability`, `soundness-compendium`, `mechanized-soundness`

### Security / undefined-behavior red team

**Expertise:** C UB exploitation, sanitizer evidence, disclosure quality.

**Red-team questions:**
1. Are the flagship UB classes security-relevant and not overclaimed?
1. Can a maintainer reproduce a witness from the disclosure materials?
1. Do sanitizer and checked-contract paths cover the claimed bug class?
1. Are non-CVE weakness replays labelled honestly?

**Evidence:** `disclosures`, `zoo`, `soundness-compendium`, `artifact-appendix`

### Systems / artifact-evaluation dry run

**Expertise:** Open-source packaging, reproducibility, containerized execution.

**Red-team questions:**
1. Can a fresh evaluator identify the exact commands to run?
1. Does the artifact state satisfy open licence, citation, and packaging checks?
1. Are reproduction commands real repository entry points?
1. Is the paper PDF built from checked-in source?

**Evidence:** `artifact-appendix`, `reproduction-kit`, `dockerfile`, `paper-source`, `paper-pdf`
